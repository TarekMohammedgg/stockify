# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Admin (owner), Cashiers (front-of-house), Delivery staff, and Customers. The staff context is high-pressure restaurant operations requiring fast scanning and minimal clicks, often using low-quality screens or tablets.

## Product Purpose
An Arabic-first, cash-only web-based restaurant management system. It handles onsite, takeaway, and delivery orders with an integrated AI chatbot for customer ordering. It exists to reduce manual order errors, speed up cashier workflows, and give admins real-time inventory and KPI visibility.

## Brand Personality
Modern, Fast, Human.
Stockify feels like a modern restaurant operating system: clean, realtime, Arabic-first, and operationally efficient. Built for restaurant staff under pressure, not enterprise bureaucracy.
References: Stripe (clean dashboards), Linear (smooth UX & motion), Toast POS / Square Restaurants (workflows), Jahez / HungerStation (modern Arabic UX).

## Anti-references
- Generic corporate SaaS / dense ERP systems
- Old Bootstrap admin panels / Material Design defaults
- Cluttered dashboards with tiny text, weak contrast, or too many colors
- Crypto/neon cyberpunk UI or over-animated interfaces
- Deep nested navigation or hover-dependent interactions

## Design Principles
1. **Speed over flair**: Optimize for fast scanning and minimal clicks in cashier workflows.
2. **Clarity under pressure**: Use clear status labels, color-blind-safe indicators, and low cognitive load layouts.
3. **Arabic-first natively**: Treat RTL and Arabic typography as the primary experience, not an afterthought.
4. **Resilient UI**: Ensure the interface works flawlessly on low-quality restaurant screens and tablets with 44x44px minimum touch targets.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.2 AA target.
- RTL-first experience with Arabic typography optimization.
- Full keyboard navigation and proper focus states.
- High contrast support and color-blind-safe status indicators.
- Responsive tablet support and reduced motion support.
